About our Radio

Justice FM 98.5 MOTO: The wise voice from the East of Freetown covers a wide range of media broadcasting and entertainment.

We have captured the Est end municipality with the popular morning program *JUSTICE & KEVIN show* and also the evening show *WI VOICE*

We give well researched and balanced stories. We embark on development oriented programing. We are interactive and easily accessible. We are registered with IMC and NATCOM and Affiliate to IRN - We are SERIOUS media

Why Choose Us?

We are certainly your first choice in the East Coast because we have a wider coverage beyound Freetown.

We also reach our audiences accross the globe with our INTERNET RADIO and Oline TV
